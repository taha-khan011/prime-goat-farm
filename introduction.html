A Legacy of Quality Breeding

Rooted in tradition, powered by modern expertise. Discover the journey
of Pakistan's premier livestock breeding farm.

SINCE 1998

Our Journey Started with a Vision for Excellence

Prime Goat Farm was founded on a simple principle: high-quality livestock deserves a high-quality life. What started as a small family barn has evolved into a state-of-the-art breeding center. We believe that healthy, happy animals produce the finest meat and milk, and we've dedicated over two decades to perfecting our care protocols and genetic lineages.

🌿

100% Organic Feed

All our livestock are raised on a specialized diet of organic silage, grains, and fresh seasonal greens for optimal development.

🛡️

Vaccination Protocol

Strict adherence to government and international health standards. Every animal is tagged and digitally tracked for health history.

❤️

Professional Care

Our team of on-site veterinarians and experienced herdsmen monitor our animals 24/7 to ensure maximum comfort and well-being.

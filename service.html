Quality Feed

Our livestock is raised on a strictly monitored diet of organic grains, fresh alfalfa, and nutrient-dense supplements, ensuring optimal growth and health.

Professional CareTakers

Dedicated animal specialists monitor our farm 24/7. Our staff is trained in modern husbandry techniques to provide a stress-free environment for all animals.

High-Quality Animals

We take pride in offering livestock that meets the highest standards of physical health, weight, and temperament, perfect for breeding or personal use.

Proper Vaccination

Complete medical history for every animal. We strictly follow WHO-standard vaccination protocols and regular deworming schedules under vet supervision.

Premium Breeds

Exotic and local breeds including Beetal, Kamori, and Gulabi goats, as well as high-yield cattle breeds, selected for their superior genetics and performance.

Health Consulting

Need advice on starting your own farm? Our experts provide consultation on shed design, feed management, and livestock procurement strategies.
